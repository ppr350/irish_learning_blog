MY IRISH LEARNING BLOG
About Me And Why I love The Irish Language
The first time Irish language really got my attention was the animation "Amhrán na Mara (Song of the Sea)", and its theme song "Amhrán Na Farraige (Song of the Sea)". The movie was wonderful, it made me want to be able to speak Irish for the first time (spoiler: I still can't do it yet).

(23 Feabhra 2023) Saw TG4 presenter Caitlín Nic Aoidh interviewed actor Paul Mescal on the BAFTA's red carpet, it felt so lovely to see it blown up, I truly think Gaeilge is in now.

(25 Eanáir 2023) Yesterday "An Cailín Ciúin" has become the first Irish language feature film to be nominated for an Oscar. I adore this movie and was delighted by the news.

Máthairchomhlacht = Parent Company

"Mathair" is "Mother" and "Comhlacht" is "Company", therefore "Mathairchomhlacht" is "Parent Company".

Smashing = is maith é sin

from reddit user u/AbsolutShite, the adjective "Smashing" is derived from Irish "Is maith é sin", which means "That is good".
