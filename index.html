MY IRISH LEARNING BLOG

About Me And Why I love The Irish Language and other things

The first time Irish language really got my attention was the animation "Amhrán na Mara (Song of the Sea)", and its theme song "Amhrán Na Farraige (Song of the Sea)". The movie was wonderful, it made me want to be able to speak Irish for the first time (spoiler: I still can't do it yet).

(4 Deireadh Fómhair 2023) My cat Wanda has lost since 1st of October, I really miss her and I hope she will find her way home.

(13 Lúil 2023) I'm taking driving lessons now so I'm trying to learn some new words about driving.

(5 Lúil 2023) I try to learn words in Irish that I come across in my everyday life, places such as news articles, instagram posts, YouTube videos, things that I see or use everyday. Yes to all!

(30 Mheitheamh 2023) I concentrate on learning vocabulary in Irish instead of trying to learn Irish grammar for now. I think the grammar is extremely hard for me (at least for now). My go to method to learn Irish now is read RTÉ nuacht / Tuairisc + listen to Raidió na Gaeltachta to pick up more words, and hope that I can understand the grammar along the way.

(23 Feabhra 2023) Saw TG4 presenter Caitlín Nic Aoidh interviewed actor Paul Mescal on the BAFTA's red carpet, it felt so lovely to see it blown up, I truly think Gaeilge is in now.

(25 Eanáir 2023) Yesterday "An Cailín Ciúin" has become the first Irish language feature film to be nominated for an Oscar. I adore this movie and was delighted by the news.
