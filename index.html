(5 Lúil 2023) I try to learn words in Irish that I come across in my everyday life, places such as news articles, instagram posts, YouTube videos, things that I see or use everyday. Yes to all!

(30 Mheitheamh 2023) I concentrate on learning vocabulary in Irish instead of trying to learn Irish grammar for now. I think the grammar is extremely hard for me (at least for now).
